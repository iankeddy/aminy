Our Mission

Aminy exists to make life easier. We believe everyone deserves access to reliable, fairly-priced local services — and that skilled helpers deserve steady, dignified work.

We are building Kenya's most trusted platform for local errands and home services, starting with Nairobi and expanding across the country.

How It Works

Browse Gigs Find verified helpers for any task — cleaning, delivery, repairs, and more.
Chat First Message before committing. Agree on details, timeline, and price.
Hire Safely Every helper is vetted with ID, selfie, and a Certificate of Good Conduct.
Rate & Review Leave honest feedback to help the community find the best helpers.

For Helpers

Join Aminy as a Helper to advertise your services, take on jobs near you, and grow your income. Create multiple gigs for each skill you offer — cleaning, gardening, tutoring, and more.

Getting started is free. Complete our quick vetting process and your profile goes live on the marketplace the same day.
